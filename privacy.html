Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

What data we collect

  • Account info: Your email address and a securely hashed password.
  • Calendar data: Events, dates, and notes you enter into the calendar.

How we use it

  • To log you into your account.
  • To display your calendar events back to you.
  • To provide customer support if you need help.

What we don't do

  • We do not sell or share your data with anyone.
  • We do not read your calendar entries unless you ask us for support.
  • We do not track you across other websites.

Data security

  • Passwords are hashed by Supabase Auth — we never see your plain-text password.
  • Row-Level Security ensures you can only see your own calendar data.
  • Your data is stored in a secure, encrypted database.

Your control

  • You can delete any event at any time.
  • You can request account deletion by contacting us.
  • You can export your data at any time.

Contact

If you have questions, reach out to us at prairie-pork@proton.me.
